About Sunshine Hospital

Our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence in healthcare

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Sunshine Hospital began as a small community clinic with a vision to provide compassionate, high-quality healthcare to our neighbors. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service medical center serving the entire region, but we've never lost sight of our original mission.

Today, Sunshine Hospital is a 250-bed facility with over 50 medical specialties and a staff of more than 1,000 healthcare professionals. We're proud to be recognized as one of the top hospitals in the state for patient satisfaction and clinical outcomes.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, high-quality healthcare that brings light to our patients' lives through innovative treatments, personalized care, and a commitment to excellence in all we do.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in our region, known for clinical excellence, patient-centered care, and a warm, welcoming environment that makes healthcare experiences positive.
